# 👨‍💼 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to help HR teams monitor workforce performance, employee demographics, and attrition trends. The dashboard provides executive-level insights that support strategic workforce planning and data-driven HR decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall workforce performance.
- Analyze employee attrition trends.
- Understand employee demographics.
- Identify departments with high attrition.
- Support HR decision-making using data.
- Improve employee retention strategies.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- SQL
- Excel

# 🔍 Key Insights

## 👨‍💼 Workforce Overview

- The organization has **1,470 employees**, with **1,233 active employees**.
- Overall employee attrition rate is **16.12%**.
- The average employee age is **37 years**.
- Employees stay with the company for an average of **7 years**.

---

## 👥 Employee Demographics

- Life Sciences is the largest education field.
- Most employees are married.
- The majority of employees travel rarely for work.
- Sales department has the highest average salary.
- Most employees have 0–10 years of experience.

---

## 📉 Attrition Insights

- Employees with lower Work-Life Balance show higher attrition.
- Lower Job Satisfaction is associated with increased attrition.
- Sales department experiences the highest attrition.
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives have the highest employee turnover.
- Employees with fewer years since their last promotion are more likely to leave.
- Relationship and Environment Satisfaction influence employee retention.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve employee work-life balance initiatives.
- Focus on employee engagement and job satisfaction.
- Create targeted retention programs for Sales and Laboratory teams.
- Increase career growth opportunities through promotions.
- Monitor high-risk employee groups proactively.
- Conduct regular employee satisfaction surveys.
